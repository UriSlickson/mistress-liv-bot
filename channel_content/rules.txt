RULES

**Advance**
- Daily by 11p est or after all games are accounted for.

**Communication**
- If member does not communicate by 5p est, they are subject to be AP'd.
- Checking out, going aloof, etc may result in removal and forfeiture of payouts. Must stay active.

**Coaches**
- Must use a Created Coach, unless joined after szn 3.
- Cannot fire coaches or coordinators.

**Rosters**
- No Roster Position Changes
- Can start a player where accessible in the depth chart, excluding K/P at QB, or QB at WR
- Only (1) mentor allowed per position.
- Cannot add a 71+ to psquad then back to roster to resign them.
- All player edits must be streamed.
- No editing Throwing Arms (Right or Left) or Throwing Style.
- Created player name changes ok but must be real names not nicknames, college, equipment & jersey # changes ok.

**Coaching Abilities**
- Camp Counselor is a banned coaching ability.
- Must show coaching abilities used when streaming prior to entering the game.
- When rewarded a development upgrade in training, please provide with a screen shot proving camp counsellor was not used.
- If it is found this was used, players will be downgraded at commissioners discretion based on research of affects.

**Playbooks**
- No Custom or Live playbooks
- Must show playbook when streaming.

**Trades**
- No trade deadline.
- Trades are off during the resigning stage.
- No trading w/ CPU.
- No trading amongst Mav/Goose partners.
- Only trade assets on the game. There is no other form of transaction for trades in the competition.
- Cannot trade draft picks the last szn of the franchise.

**Streaming**
- When streaming a game must post the link in #townsquare, the game channel, & DM the opponent.
- No pausing once a game begins.
- Streams must be saved, and/or provided upon request.
- Home Team streams, if unable, visitor has option, if neither can stream it's a Fair Sim.
- (1) start per game, must resume if disconnected.
- Injuries stay on for games that result in a FW/FL scenario, which will only occur for disconnects that didn't reconnect.
